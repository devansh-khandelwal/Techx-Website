Rules and Regulations

Discipline is the bridge between
goals and accomplishment.

InnovateNSUT

Code Wars

Quiz

Teams
You can have at most 6 members in your team. You can either invite your friends to form a team or you can request other teams to add you as a member.
Lone Wolfs are also allowed
Mode
It's an OnCampus Offline Hackathon.
Duration
The Duration of the Hackathon is 24 hours. Hack phase is of 24 hours. Presentation and Final round is of 4-5 hours
Innovation
You are expected to come up with new and innovative ideas, any idea that has been copied from somewhere will be disqualified.
Themes
Multiple themes will be released soon, you must submit a hack of these themes only.
Submission
Once the hackathon starts, Entries can be submitted anytime in the 24 hour hack phase.
Development
Your hack must be developed entirely during the Hackathon duration. You may use open source libraries and other freely available systems / services such as Google Maps, Facebook Connect, Twitter feeds etc.
Intellectual Property
The intellectual property of your code belongs only to your team. By participating in the hackathon, you agree to the terms and conditions of Devfolio.
Teams
Only Lone Wolfs are allowed, Candidates have to participate individually.
Mode
CodeWars is an Offline coding contest which will be conducted on HackerRank.
Duration
The Contest is of 3 hours.
Languages
C, C++, Java and Python are allowed.
Scoring & Ranking
Each challenge has a pre-determined score and a participant’s score depends on the number of test cases successfully passed (partial scores given for passing partial testcases).
If a participant submits more than one solution per challenge, then the score will reflect the highest score achieved.
Participants are ranked by score. If two or more participants achieve the same score, then the tie is broken by the total time taken to submit the last solution resulting in a higher score.
Submission
Participants can submit solution to a problem any number of times, the solution with best score will be considered.
Immediate disqualification in case of any plagiarism.
The decision of organizing committee will be final.
Platform
The Contest will be conducted on HackerRank. Online compiler of HackerRank will be available and you can also write your code in your IDE and submit on the Website.
Colleges allowed
All colleges of Delhi-NCR are allowed to take part in the Contest.
Teams
Only Lone Wolfs are allowed, Candidates have to participate individually.
Mode
The Quiz will be held in Offline mode on a Google form.
Duration
The Quiz will be of 3 hours. The quiz will start at 12PM sharp.
Theme
Quiz questions will be General Tech based.
So, all the tech geeks Gear up and Get ready for this Amazing Challenge!!!
Submission
You have to submit your responses in the given time of 3 hours.
Incase of a tie, Submission time will be taken as a Tie Breaker.
Scoring & Ranking
The Score will be calculated on the basis of number of questions correctly answered. For each correct answer 10 points will be awarded. There is no negative marking.
Top 3 participants will be awarded with prizes/vouchers worth ______.

Event Guideline

IOSD NSUT Presents TechX’22

TechX’22 is the first pan-India technical fest by International Organisation of Software Developers - Netaji Subhash University of Technology (esrtwhile NSIT) Building upon the success of IOSD’s own hackathons in various chapters in colleges across India, TechX’22 is a result of innovation and an aspiration to dream higher. A completely online event, TechX will be held on various online platforms like Devfolio and {{Platform of CodeWars}} to make it accessible to all students amidst the pandemic.

TechX’22 comprises of 3 events :

Check List

  • InnovateNSUT - A hackathon conducted on HackerEarth
  • CodeWars - A comprehensive 2- round coding contest
  • PsychoTech - 3-hour long tech-based quiz
